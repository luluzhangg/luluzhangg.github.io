Mushroom Risotto

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Arborio rice
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 cup sliced mushrooms

    (Shiitake, Chanterelle, Oyster or Porcini are often

    recommended for making risotto)

  • 1/2 cup chopped onion
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup dry white wine
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. In a large pan, heat the olive oil over medium heat.
  2. Add the onions, garlic, and mushrooms to the pan and saut√© for 5-7 minutes, until the onions are translucent and the mushrooms are browned.
  3. Remove the mushroom mixture from the pan and set it aside.
  4. In the same pan, add the Arborio rice and stir it for 1-2 minutes, until it is coated in oil and slightly translucent.
  5. Add the white wine to the pan and stir until it has been fully absorbed by the rice.
  6. Begin adding the vegetable broth to the pan, 1/2 cup at a time, stirring constantly.
  7. Wait until each addition of broth has been fully absorbed before adding more.
  8. Repeat step 6 until the rice is cooked through and the risotto has a creamy consistency.
  9. Add the mushroom mixture back into the pan and stir it into the risotto.
  10. Mix in the butter and Parmesan cheese.
  11. Season the risotto with salt and pepper to taste.
  12. Serve the mushroom risotto hot and enjoy!
